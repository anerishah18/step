Intro to Me

Hi! I'm Aneri, an EECS major at UC Berkeley and a 2020 STEP Intern at Google. I am born and brought up in Fremont, CA, at the heart of the Bay Area and have been passionate about math and technology for as long as I can remember. In addition, I love to read fiction, try new foods, choreograph dance routines, and go camping (the picture below is from a New Years' camping trip).

At Cal, I am involved with several student organizations include SWE (Society of Women Engineers), CSM (Computer Science Mentors), and UC Berkeley Raas Ramzat (a competitive Indian dance team). These organizations encapsulate what I am passionate about outside academic pursuits directly tied to my degree, including teaching, community activities, and dance.
